here are audio-files as per the [XDG soundtheme namingconvention](https://0pointer.de/public/sound-naming-spec.html) as described in [XR Fragment level 6](https://xrfragment.org/#%F0%9F%93%9Clevel6%3A%20XDG%20soundtheme)

> NOTE: to comply with the spec, and to keep the default XDG-soundtheme <60kb, many events are symlinked to `generic*.mp3`. Though webui apps or janusrooms are welcome to bring their own.

In case of introducing new sounds, keep the filesize small use these mp3 encoding-settings:

* bright sounds: 112kbps 44100hz at constant bitrate
* low sounds: 50kpbs <12500hz at variable bitrate
